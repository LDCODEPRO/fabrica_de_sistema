# DEVOPS_AGENT — MASTERS AND REFERENCES

## Purpose
Key thinkers and organizations whose work defines the DevOps and SRE body of knowledge that informs the DEVOPS_AGENT.

---

## Gene Kim

**Role:** Co-founder of Tripwire; IT management researcher; author and conference organizer (DevOps Enterprise Summit).

**Key works:**
- Co-author of "The Phoenix Project" (IT Revolution Press, 2013) — with Kevin Behr and George Spafford.
- Lead author of "The DevOps Handbook" (IT Revolution Press, 2016) — with Jez Humble, Patrick Debois, John Willis.
- Co-author of "Accelerate" (IT Revolution Press, 2018) — with Nicole Forsgren and Jez Humble.

**Core ideas:**
- The Three Ways of DevOps: Flow, Feedback, Continual Learning.
- Identifying and eliminating constraints in the software delivery value stream.
- Connecting IT performance to organizational performance via the DORA research program.

---

## Jez Humble

**Role:** Principal scientist at DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment); formerly at ThoughtWorks and Google Cloud.

**Key works:**
- Co-author of "Continuous Delivery" (Addison-Wesley, 2010) — with David Farley.
- Co-author of "The DevOps Handbook" (2016).
- Co-author of "Accelerate" (2018) — with Nicole Forsgren and Gene Kim.
- Co-author of "Lean Enterprise" (O'Reilly, 2015) — with Joanne Molesky and Barry O'Reilly.

**Core ideas:**
- The deployment pipeline as a first-class engineering artifact.
- Continuous delivery as a capability, not just a tool.
- Measurement-driven improvement: DORA metrics as leading indicators of organizational performance.

---

## Patrick Debois

**Role:** Belgian IT consultant; widely credited with coining the term "DevOps" (2009, DevOpsDays Ghent).

**Key contributions:**
- Organized the first DevOpsDays conference (Ghent, Belgium, 2009).
- Co-author of "The DevOps Handbook."
- Advocate for breaking down silos between development, QA, and operations teams.

---

## John Willis

**Role:** IT industry analyst; formerly at Docker, Chef, Red Hat.

**Key works:**
- Co-author of "The DevOps Handbook."
- Speaker and educator on DevOps culture, Lean IT, and W. Edwards Deming's management philosophy.

**Core ideas:** Applied Deming's System of Profound Knowledge to IT operations.

---

## David Farley

**Role:** Software engineer, consultant, and author; formerly at LMAX Exchange.

**Key works:**
- Co-author of "Continuous Delivery" (Addison-Wesley, 2010) — with Jez Humble.
- Author of "Modern Software Engineering" (Addison-Wesley, 2022).
- Popular YouTube channel (Continuous Delivery) on software engineering and CD practices.

**Core ideas:** Treating software delivery as an engineering discipline; the deployment pipeline; test automation strategy.

---

## Betsy Beyer, Chris Jones, Jennifer Petoff, Niall Richard Murphy

**Role:** Site Reliability Engineers at Google.

**Key works:**
- Co-editors and authors of "Site Reliability Engineering: How Google Runs Production Systems" (O'Reilly, 2016) — the foundational SRE text.
- Co-authors of "The Site Reliability Workbook" (O'Reilly, 2018) — practical companion with more implementation guidance.

**Core ideas:**
- SLIs, SLOs, and error budgets as engineering tools for reliability.
- Eliminating toil through automation.
- Blameless postmortems.
- The concept that SRE is "what happens when you ask a software engineer to design an operations function."

---

## Nicole Forsgren

**Role:** Researcher; formerly at DORA (which she co-founded), Google Cloud; VP Research at GitHub.

**Key works:**
- Lead author and CEO of DORA.
- Co-author of "Accelerate: The Science of Lean Software and DevOps" (IT Revolution Press, 2018) — with Jez Humble and Gene Kim.

**Core ideas:**
- Empirical measurement of software delivery performance.
- DORA metrics: deployment frequency, lead time for changes, mean time to restore (MTTR), change failure rate.
- Psychological safety as a predictor of team performance.

---

## Nigel Poulton

**Role:** Technical author and educator; Docker Captain.

**Key works:**
- "Docker Deep Dive" (self-published/Leanpub, regularly updated) — practical guide to Docker containerization.
- "The Kubernetes Book" — accessible introduction to Kubernetes concepts.

---

## Kevin Behr & George Spafford

**Role:** IT management consultants.

**Key works:**
- Co-authors of "The Phoenix Project" (IT Revolution Press, 2013) — with Gene Kim. Also co-authored the precursor "The Visible Ops Handbook."
